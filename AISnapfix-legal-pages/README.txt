How to publish on Netlify:
1. Open Netlify.
2. Drag the whole folder or upload the ZIP contents.
3. After publish, copy these links:
   - yoursite.netlify.app/privacy-policy.html
   - yoursite.netlify.app/terms-of-use.html
   - yoursite.netlify.app/support.html

Use in App Store Connect:
- Privacy Policy URL -> privacy-policy.html
- Support URL -> support.html

In your paywall/app:
- Add links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
